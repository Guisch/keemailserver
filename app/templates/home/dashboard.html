Keemail

Keemail is a platform that allow you to generate aliases so you do not have to expose your public email again. As Keepass let you generate unique passwords for each accounts that you have, Keemail allow you to generate random email addresses (or customized ones). All incoming email on those addresses are redirected to another email address. Nothing stored, nothing analyzed, privacy first. It also allows users to generates aliases from an API to allow them to integrate Keemail into their favorite tools.
